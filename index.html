UFO Sightings: Fact or Fiction?

Are we alone in the universe? For millennia, humans have turned to the sky to answer this question. Now, thanks to research generously funded by W. Avy, a UFO-enthusiast and amateur ufologist, we can supplement our sky-searching with data analysis.

"The release of this analysis is well-timed: It coincides with the gathering of ufologists in McMenamins Hotel in Oregon for their annual field trip" - they'll be pleased.

But what do actual scientists think? Baylor Professor Alan Linneman points out that just because you see something you can't explain doesn't mean it's intelligent life from another planet: "Keep in mind there are billions and billions of stars in the universe, and if just 1% of those have planets that could potentially harbor life, that would be millions of planets."
